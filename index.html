FAQ

What is this?

Zero5s is an AI chat workspace. You can run multiple AI chats side by side, upload files, and switch between different AI models — all in one place.

How do I add more chats?

Click the + Add Chat button in the top toolbar. Each chat is fully independent with its own history, memory, and settings.

How do I delete a chat?

Click the x button in the top right corner of any chat box.

What AI models are available?

You can pick from four providers in each chat: OpenRouter (auto-picks best free model), Groq (Llama 3.3 70B — fast and smart), Cerebras (Llama 3.1 8B — ultra fast), and Mistral (multiple models).

How do I upload a file?

Click the + button next to the message input. Supported file types include PDF, Word (.docx), Excel, CSV, images, and most code/text files. The AI will read the file and answer questions about it.

What does the creativity slider do?

It controls how creative or random the AI's responses are. Low values (0.0–0.4) give more focused, predictable answers. High values (1.0–2.0) make responses more varied and creative. 0.7 is a good default.

How does memory work?

Each chat tracks basic facts you mention about yourself (like your name or interests) and includes them in future messages so the AI remembers context. Click clear to wipe a chat's memory.

Can I export my conversations?

Yes — click the export button on any chat to download the full conversation as a .txt file.

How do I change the look of the app?

Click the 🎨 Theme button to open the theme panel. You can pick from 6 color themes, change the text color with swatches or a custom color picker, and adjust the font size.

Can I resize the chat boxes?

Yes — drag the bottom-right corner of any chat box to resize it however you want.

Can I rename a chat?

Yes — click directly on the chat title text at the top of any chat box and type a new name.

Does it save my chats?

Theme, text color, and font size preferences are saved automatically. Chat history itself is not saved between page refreshes — export important conversations before closing.
